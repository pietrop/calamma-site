Welcome

Peaceful country living, minutes from the city

The recently renovated stone house offers spacious, bright and cool interiors, comfortably sleeping up to six guests: two double bedrooms, plus a cosy mini living room upstairs with a double sofa bed for two more. The natural surroundings make it an ideal destination for hiking, river swimming and al fresco dining, while Bologna, Florence and Venice are within easy reach for day trips.

Location

In the hills, close to everything

Ca'Lamma sits in a quiet woodland valley in Monterenzio, on the Apennine hills south of Bologna. Days here start with birdsong and end with valley sunsets, yet swimming pools, spas, trattorias and three of Italy's great art cities are an easy drive away. Tennis courts, horse riding and local museums are just a few kilometres from the door.
